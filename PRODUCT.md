# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

TRAFFODATA speaks to operators, founders, and technical decision-makers who need serious ERP, inventory, warehouse, POS, accounting, CRM, and HR systems. They are evaluating whether the team can understand operational pressure and ship dependable software, not browsing a decorative agency portfolio.

## Product Purpose

The site presents TRAFFODATA as a premium operational software partner. It should make the company feel technically capable, visually refined, and reliable under business-critical conditions. The work pages must prove delivery through consistent case-study structure, strong media, and clear system detail.

## Brand Personality

Precise, composed, and system-minded. TRAFFODATA should feel like premium B2B infrastructure: dark-tech, confident, restrained, and operationally fluent rather than loud or ornamental.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI-purple gradients, random stock-like media, empty oversized agency layouts, decorative dashboards without meaning, over-rounded cards, cheap loading spinners, and animation that harms readability or performance. Do not weaken or remove the existing liquid hero animation.

## Design Principles

1. Show operational systems, not abstract decoration.
2. Keep one coherent media language across every project detail page.
3. Use the purple-blue accent as product light and signal, not as generic glow.
4. Let motion feel prepared and smooth, with reduced-motion alternatives.
5. Preserve readability, accessible contrast, and disciplined spacing at every breakpoint.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain Lighthouse accessibility at 100 where possible. All controls need names and adequate touch targets. Media needs meaningful captions or accessible labeling. Motion must respect `prefers-reduced-motion`, and loading states must not trap users or create layout shift.
